Welcome to Swift Doodles
​by Marcie Swift

​I'm inspired by shapes and patterns and their soothing repetition. I look for humor in my drawings, finding comedy in the mundane. I seek to find something new, to find a place I haven't been before.  I search for the lines of sanity written on my body as I fall asleep. I listen to the quiet night air hanging stars by her toes. Art exists when I remember to forget my separateness, when I remember to notice the shadows on the wall, when I remember to leave room for possibilities.
